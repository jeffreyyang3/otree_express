General Instructions

Welcome to the LEEPS Laboratory. During the course of this session, you will be asked to make a series of decisions. By participating in this study, you will earn real money that will be paid to you in cash at the end of the experiment. On top of what you earn during the session, you will receive a participation fee of {{ participation_fee }}.

It is important you do not speak out loud, laugh or talk to other participants during the duration of the session. We expect and appreciate your cooperation. If you have any question, please raise your hand and an experimenter will attend to you. After you have read these instructions, you will answer a few brief questions to ensure you understand them.


Stages

This session consists of two stages. In Stage 1, called Task Stage, you will work on a set of tasks to earn money. We will refer to the earned amount as the task income. Specific instructions for these tasks will be provided once Stage 1 starts.

{# Stage 2: general | All but TP #} {% if treatment != 'TP' %}

In Stage 2, called Interaction Stage, you will interact with another participant in the room. First, you will be anonymously paired with one another. You will then be randomly assigned to be either of Role A or of Role B. You will remain in the same role throughout the entire duration of the experiment. You will be informed about your role on your computer screen once Stage 2 starts. You will not be informed about the identity of the person you are paired with, neither during nor after the experiment.


Role A:

If you are assigned Role A, you are to decide how much of B’s task income you would like to transfer into your own account. You are able to transfer anywhere from 0 to 100 percent of B’s task income, but you will not be able to transfer B’s endowment. The image below shows the screen where you will make your decision:



Role B:

If you are assigned Role B, you will be asked to guess what percentage of your task income A will transfer to his/her own account.

{% endif %} {# Stage 2: general | only TP #} {% if treatment == 'TP' or role == 'TP-R' %}

In Stage 2, called Interaction Stage, there are three possible roles for each participant, Role A, Role B and Role R. First, one participant will be randomly assigned Role R. The rest of participants will be anonymously paired in groups of two. Within each group, roles will be randomly assigned so that there will be one person in Role A and one in Role B. You will remain in the same role throughout the entire duration of the experiment, and will be informed about your role on your computer screen once Stage 2 starts. You will not be informed about the identity of the person you are paired with, neither during nor after the experiment.

Role R:

If you are assigned Role R (Reader) your only task is to read the messages written by participants in Role B who are able to send a message to you. The Reader is the only participant that is going to read these messages. None of the other participants in today's session will ever read them. The Reader will not receive any information about participants of Roles A or B. After reading the messages, the Reader will confirm having read them.

Role A:

If you are assigned Role A, you are to decide how much of B’s task income you would like to transfer into your own account. You are able to transfer anywhere from 0 to 100 percent of B’s task income, but you will not be able to transfer B’s endowment. The image below shows the screen where you will make your decision:



Role B:

If you are assigned Role B, you will be asked to guess what percentage of your task income the participant in Role A will transfer to his/her own account.

{# paid messages: DM/TP WTA/WTP BDMCONT #} {% if value_type == 'WTP' and elicitation_method == 'BDM' and BDM_type == 'CONT' %}

You will be asked to state the maximum amount that you are willing to pay to send your message. We will refer to this amount as your valuation.

After you submit your valuation, you will be informed about the actual price of sending your message. The actual price is randomly generated by the computer, and all numbers between $0 and {{ BDM_uplimit }} are equally likely.

If your valuation is greater than or equal to the actual price, your message will be sent, and you will pay the actual price. If your valuation is lower than the actual price, then you do not get to send your message, and you will not be charged.


{# reviewed with brit up to this point. Nov 03#} {% elif value_type == 'WTA' and elicitation_method == 'BDM' and BDM_type == 'CONT' %}

You will be asked to state the minimum amount that you are willing to accept for not sending your message. That is, your will report the least amount you would be willing to receive to forgo sending your message. We will refer to this amount as your valuation.

After you submit your valuation, you will be informed about the actual compensation you will receive for not sending your message. The actual compensation is randomly generated by the computer, and all numbers between $0 and {{ BDM_uplimit }} are equally likely.

If the actual compensation is lower than your valuation, your message will be delivered and you will not receive any compensation. When the actual compensation is greater than or equal to your valuation, your message will not be delivered, and you will receive the actual compensation.


{% endif %} {# paid messages: WTA/WTP BDMLIST #} {% if value_type == 'WTP' and elicitation_method == 'BDM' and BDM_type == 'LIST' %}

You will be asked whether you are willing to pay a certain price to send your message. This question will be repeated for different prices.

After you submit your responses, the computer will randomly select one of the prices listed. If you were willing to pay the selected price, your message will be sent, and you will pay the selected price. If you were not willing to pay the selected price, your message will not be sent, and you will not be charged.


{% elif value_type == 'WTA' and elicitation_method == 'BDM' and BDM_type == 'LIST' %}

You will be asked whether you are willing to accept a certain amount in exchange for not sending the message. This question will be repeated for different amounts of compensation.

After you submit your responses, the computer will randomly select one of the amounts of compensation listed. If you were willing to accept the selected amount, your message will not be sent, and you will receive the selected compensation. If you were not willing to accept the selected amount, your message will be sent, and you will not receive any compensation.


{% endif %} {# paid messages: WTA/WTP SOP #} {% if value_type == 'WTP' and elicitation_method == 'SOP' %}

You will be asked whether you are willing to pay a certain price to send your message. If you respond positively, your message will be sent, and you will pay that price. If you respond negatively, your message will not be sent, and you will not pay anything.


{% elif value_type == 'WTA' and elicitation_method == 'SOP' %}

You will be asked whether you are willing to accept a certain amount in exchange for not sending your message. If you respond positively, your message will not be sent, and you will receive the certain amount as compensation. If you respond negatively, your message will be sent, and you will not receive any compensation.


{% endif %} {% endif %} {#################################################################} {# everyone PAYOFFS and closing #}

Final Earnings:

After this interaction, members of each pair will be informed about both participants' final earnings, which will be calculated as follows.


Role A's earnings =

Endowment + Task income + Money taken from B's account.


{% if treatment == 'NM' or treatment == 'FM'%}

Role B's earnings =

Endowment + Task income - Money taken by Role A.


{% endif %} {% if treatment == 'DM' or treatment == 'TP' %} {% if value_type == 'WTP' %}

Role B's earnings when message is not sent =

Endowment + Task income - Money taken by Role A.


{% endif %} {% if value_type == 'WTA' %}

Role B's earnings when message is sent =

Endowment + Task income - Money taken by Role A.


{% endif %} {% if treatment == 'TP' %}

Role R's earnings =

Endowment + Task income.

{% endif %} {% endif %}

Note that final earnings do not include your participation fee.
